How to Use & Future Vision

How to Use This Prototype

  1. Generate a Briefing: Select an advisor from the dropdown and click "Generate Briefing" to see their profile and AI-generated talking points.
  2. Open the AI Assistant: Click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner to open the conversational assistant.
  3. Ask Follow-Up Questions: With a briefing active, you can ask the assistant to perform tasks. Try these prompts:
    • "Draft a follow-up email for her."
    • "Summarize the key takeaways."
    • "What is a likely objection she might have to our ESG fund?"

Future Enhancements: A Strategic Roadmap

This prototype is the foundation. Here is how this tool would evolve into an indispensable strategic asset:

  • Deeper Data Integration:Connect to a live data warehouse to analyze real-time portfolio holdings, trading activity, and sentiment from meeting transcripts.
  • Comparative Analysis:Enable queries like "Compare Sarah Chen and David Miller" to get AI-driven insights on which advisor represents a better near-term opportunity.
  • Proactive Alerts:Transform the tool from reactive to proactive. Wholesalers would receive alerts when an AI agent detects a trigger event (e.g., a competitor's fund in an advisor's portfolio is downgraded).
  • "Close the Loop" CRM Logging:Add a "Log to CRM" button that uses AI to generate a perfect, structured summary of the entire interaction, ensuring valuable intelligence is captured consistently.
